# Source: Apple — App Review Guidelines, Business

- **Author:** Apple
- **Published:** Current App Review Guidelines (retrieved 2026-07-11)
- **URL:** https://developer.apple.com/app-store/review/guidelines/
- **Retrieved:** 2026-07-11

## Summary

Apple's current guidelines require in-app purchase for digital features, levels, virtual currency, and
subscriptions in the covered cases. They require purchased in-app currencies not to expire and require
randomized virtual-item purchases such as loot boxes to disclose item odds before purchase. Apple also
expects the business model and purchases to be clear to reviewers and customers. The rules make
monetization presentation, restore behavior, and probability disclosure part of product design.
