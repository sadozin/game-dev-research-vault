## Summary

The Blender Manual describes Unwrap as flattening a mesh along seams and offers projection methods for
simpler or more specialized surfaces. It documents angle-based and conformal approaches, aspect
correction, hole handling, and island margins. The resulting coordinates need not fill the entire image,
and faces may intentionally share portions of a texture.
