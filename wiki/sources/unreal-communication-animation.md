## Summary

Unreal offers direct references, casts, interfaces, and event dispatchers for communication, while
Animation Montages combine sequences into controllable sections, slots, and events. These mechanisms
make relationships explicit when chosen well; indiscriminate casting and montage side effects create
coupling that is hard to test or replicate.
