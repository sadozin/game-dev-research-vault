## Summary

Battle passes turn a season into a visible progression track with recurring goals and a predictable
reward cadence. Their health depends on achievable pacing, meaningful free rewards, and a clear
season boundary; excessive grind or paywalled completion converts engagement into obligation. The
same economy should be checked for sinks, inventory friction, and whether rewards compete with the
core game rather than reinforce it.
