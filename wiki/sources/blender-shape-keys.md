# Source: Blender Manual — Shape Keys

- **Author:** Blender Foundation

## Summary

Blender distinguishes relative and absolute shape keys. Relative keys define a shape against the Basis
or another reference and mix through weighted offsets, making them suitable for facial animation and
muscle or joint corrections. Absolute keys interpolate through shapes over evaluation time. The
manual also describes creating component shapes from a mixed extreme pose to reduce conflicts.
