## What the source says

Legion TD 2 presents multiplayer tower defense as a three-way tension between holding incoming
waves, sending offensive pressure to opponents, and investing in economy for the late game.

## Derived production implication

Send-creep PvP is not only classic defense with networking. The same resources shape defense,
offense, and growth, so adversarial timing and snowball recovery become the central economy design.
