## Summary

Blender mesh tools edit topology directly, Geometry Nodes builds procedural geometry and attributes,
and modifiers provide non-destructive operations in an ordered stack. These workflows accelerate
iteration and variation, but export requires deliberate application, evaluated-mesh checks, naming,
scale, normals, and performance decisions.
