## What the source says

Dungeon Defenders is marketed as a tower-defense action RPG with direct combat, character classes,
loot, cooperative play, and varied level layouts.

## Derived production implication

A game that actually ships that advertised combination must support networking, character combat,
loot balance, camera/control feel, enemy behavior, and tower simulation. This scope conclusion is
derived from the feature package; it is not a claim made by the store description.
