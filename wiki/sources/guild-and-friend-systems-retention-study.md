# Source: Hwang and Han — Guild and Friend Systems in MMORPGs

- **Author:** Insung Hwang and Kunsoo Han
- **Published:** 2023
- **URL:** https://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/papers.cfm?abstract_id=4606486
- **Retrieved:** 2026-07-11

## Summary

The study examines guild and friend systems and their relationship to user retention in an MMORPG. It
frames social-system design as a retention question that can be studied from player behavior rather than
assuming every social feature has the same effect.
