# Source: METR AI developer productivity study and update

- **Author:** METR
- **Published:** 2025-07-10; update 2026-02-24
- **URLs:** https://metr.org/blog/2025-07-10-early-2025-ai-experienced-os-dev-study/ and https://metr.org/blog/2026-02-24-uplift-update/
- **Raw:** raw/metr-ai-developer-productivity.md (provenance only)

## Summary

METR randomized 246 real tasks across 16 experienced open-source developers working in repositories
they knew. With early-2025 AI tools, the measured completion time increased by 19 percent even though
developers believed the tools sped them up. The study is evidence against assuming a universal AI
productivity multiplier, not evidence that AI always slows development.

METR's 2026 methodology update says newer tools likely provide more uplift and changes its experiment
design to remain informative. Tool generation, task shape, repository familiarity, measurement method,
and developer workflow all matter. MMO planning should therefore measure local cycle time and defect
escape rather than discounting a schedule by a marketing percentage.
