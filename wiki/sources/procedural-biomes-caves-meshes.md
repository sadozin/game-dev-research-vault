# Source: procedural biomes, caves, and mesh generation references

- **Author:** Amit Patel
- **Published:** continuously maintained references
- **URL:** https://www.redblobgames.com/maps/terrain-from-noise/
- **Retrieved:** 2026-07-11

## Summary

Procedural worlds become coherent when scalar fields, climate or moisture rules, connectivity, and
gameplay masks are layered instead of treating random noise as a finished map. Cellular automata can
produce cave-like regions, while runtime mesh generation converts fields into renderable geometry.
Every generator needs deterministic seeds, validation, and a budget for memory, topology, and seams.
