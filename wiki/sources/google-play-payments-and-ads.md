# Source: Google Play — Payments and Ads policies

- **Author:** Google
- **Published:** Current policy pages (retrieved 2026-07-11)
- **URL:** https://support.google.com/googleplay/android-developer/answer/9858738?hl=en
- **Retrieved:** 2026-07-11

## Summary

Google Play requires Play Billing for most digital in-app purchases, including virtual currency,
extra gameplay content, subscriptions, and ad-free functionality, subject to its listed regional
program exceptions. The payments policy also requires advance odds disclosure for randomized virtual
items. Its ads policy distinguishes explicitly opted-in rewarded ads from disruptive or unexpected
ads and adds extra requirements for apps targeting children. These are release constraints as well as
implementation details because the monetization loop determines what the purchase and ad represent.
