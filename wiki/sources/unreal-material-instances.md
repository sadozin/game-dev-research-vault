- **Published:** continuously maintained documentation

## Summary

Unreal material instances inherit from a parameterized parent and expose selected values without
duplicating the full material graph. This accelerates variation and reduces shader recompilation, but
the parent graph and parameter choices remain a shared performance and maintenance dependency.
