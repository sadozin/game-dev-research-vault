## Summary

Blender's glTF exporter supports meshes, materials, textures, cameras, lights, skins, and animation,
but maps Blender data into glTF's runtime-oriented representation. Export can triangulate quads and
split vertices for discontinuous UVs or flat edges; material support centers on recognized PBR nodes.
The exporter offers selected/visible/collection scope and packed or separate output forms.
