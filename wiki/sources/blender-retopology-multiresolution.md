- **Author:** Blender Foundation

## Summary

Blender's Multiresolution modifier supports sculpting across subdivision levels and can reproject
detail with Shrinkwrap. The manual recommends project wrapping and an above-surface snap setup for
that workflow. It also notes that the modifier has stack-order constraints, which matters when the
retopologized mesh is prepared for baking or deformation.
