# Source: Khronos glTF 2.0 specification

- **Author:** Khronos Group
- **Published:** 2017-06-27
- **URL:** https://registry.khronos.org/glTF/specs/2.0/glTF-2.0.html
- **Retrieved:** 2026-07-11

## Summary

The specification defines a portable real-time asset format, including a metallic-roughness
material model, tangent-space normal textures, and explicit color-space handling. It is useful as a
cross-engine contract, but an interchange specification does not remove engine-specific lighting,
compression, or shader differences.
