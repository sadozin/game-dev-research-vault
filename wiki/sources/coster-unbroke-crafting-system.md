# Source: Samuel Coster — How we unbroke our crafting system

- **Author:** Samuel Coster
- **Published:** 2014-08-26
- **URL:** https://www.gamedeveloper.com/design/how-we-unbroke-our-crafting-system
- **Retrieved:** 2026-07-11

## Summary

Coster describes a crafting system that exposed every station recipe too early, overwhelming players
and making them rush the station with the best progression. The redesign made recipes discoverable and
introduced them as rewards from exploration, giving the crafting system its own loop: finding a recipe
enables new builds, which creates reasons to gather and explore. The lesson is to pace recipe breadth
around player goals rather than treat a recipe list as content by itself.
