## What the source says

Robot Entertainment describes Orcs Must Die as action-oriented trap defense in which a directly
controlled War Mage uses weapons, spells, and traps against hordes; current entries also combine
roguelite choices and co-op.

## Derived production implication

Trap defense deserves its own hybrid label because surface placement, trigger/reset timing, physics,
combos, and direct combat produce a different content and performance shape from autonomous towers.
