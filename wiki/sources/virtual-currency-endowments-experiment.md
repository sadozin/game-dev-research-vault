# Source: Bapna et al. — Results of a massive experiment on virtual currency endowments

- **Author:** Bapna et al.
- **Published:** 2017
- **URL:** https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC5646808/
- **Retrieved:** 2026-07-11

## Summary

This randomized field experiment assigned different starting virtual-currency endowments to 575,000
players in a football-management game. The higher-endowment groups showed higher currency demand and
faster inflation effects, demonstrating that virtual money supply can change player behavior quickly.
It is a reason to measure economic interventions experimentally rather than infer health from a larger
balance or short-term purchase lift.
