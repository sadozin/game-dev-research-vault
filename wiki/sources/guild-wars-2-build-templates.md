## Summary

Build templates preserve a profession's selected skills, traits, and specializations as named
loadouts. Players can change them through the character panel, keybinds, or skill-bar access;
editing a skill updates the active template. Templates do not allow a build to cross profession
boundaries, so they enable situational adaptation without erasing the character's core identity.

The design separates experimentation from repetitive setup. It lets players save a solo, group,
or encounter-specific build while keeping the cost and restrictions of switching under the
designer's control.
