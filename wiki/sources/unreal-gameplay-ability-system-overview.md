# Source: Epic — Gameplay Ability System

- **Author:** Epic Games
- **Published:** Undated (Unreal Engine 5.8 documentation)

## Summary

Epic describes GAS as a framework for actors to own and trigger abilities and interactions. Its major
pieces include the Ability System Component, active and passive abilities, Attribute Sets, Gameplay
Effects, Gameplay Cues, and Ability Tasks. The documentation positions the framework for RPGs,
action-adventure games, MOBAs, and other projects where abilities coordinate mechanics with visual,
animation, and audio systems.
