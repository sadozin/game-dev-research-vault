## Summary

Project Ascension presents a classless MMO in which ability and talent selection constitutes the
character's progression. The system supports inexpensive respecs, build sharing, and an external
builder, all of which are necessary tools once the number of legal combinations becomes large.

This is a product description, not comparative research. It is useful as evidence of the
operational commitments that classless design creates: players need discovery, planning, and
recovery tools, and designers need guardrails against dominant combinations.
