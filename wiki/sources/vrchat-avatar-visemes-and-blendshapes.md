## Summary

VRChat supports defining visemes with blendshapes or a jaw-flap bone and can often detect visemes
automatically. Its avatar guidance notes that keeping blendshapes on a separate head mesh and reducing
unnecessary body-mesh keys can improve performance. The platform therefore requires both technically
correct shapes and deliberate mesh and expression budgets.
