# Source: Green — MMO trinity design

- **Author:** Brian Green

- **Retrieved:** 2026-07-11

## Summary

The tank/healer/DPS trinity is a proven way to structure damage management and group dependency,
but it is not a universal default. Tanking mitigates incoming damage, healing restores it, and DPS
ends the encounter; crowd control, buffs, melee/ranged distinctions, and single-target/AoE output
are additional roles or dimensions of those jobs.

The crucial design question is what cooperative problem the class system should create. The
trinity provides readable group composition and complementary specialization, but solo advancement
and player preference for damage can create role shortages. A design that rejects the trinity
still needs intentional substitutes for encounter coordination, social interdependence, and
class identity.
