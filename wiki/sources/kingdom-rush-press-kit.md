# Source: Kingdom Rush Press Kit

## Summary

Ironhide describes Kingdom Rush through a fixed-route defense vocabulary augmented by tower
specializations, active spells and reinforcements, commandable soldiers, heroes, enemy skills,
bosses, alternate modes, achievements, and an in-game encyclopedia. It is a useful example of how a
legible path-based foundation can support active intervention and long-form content without turning
into a player-built maze.

The large roster is the result of production maturity, not a sensible prototype target. A first
project should copy the clarity of the role structure and information surfaces, not the content count.
