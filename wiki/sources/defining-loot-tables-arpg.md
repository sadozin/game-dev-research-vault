# Source: Chris Remo — Defining Loot Tables in ARPG Game Design

- **Author:** Chris Remo
- **Published:** 2014-05-06
- **URL:** https://www.gamedeveloper.com/design/defining-loot-tables-in-arpg-game-design
- **Retrieved:** 2026-07-11

## Summary

Remo decomposes procedural loot into successive decisions: whether an item drops, its rarity, its
type, its affixes, and the numeric values of those affixes. Separating base-item and affix tables
allows variety without hardcoding every combination, but also shows why uncontrolled pools create junk
or accidental power spikes. Weighted sources and guaranteed-quality drops are presented as ways to
shape the distribution without abandoning procedural generation.
