# Source: How I Got My Mom to Play Through Plants vs. Zombies

- **Author:** George Fan, PopCap Games
- **Published:** 2012-03-09, GDC
- **URL:** https://www.gdcvault.com/play/1015541/Playtesting-for-Game
- **Retrieved:** 2026-07-14
- **Raw:** raw/plants-vs-zombies-onboarding-gdc.md (provenance only)

## Summary

Fan explains how Plants vs. Zombies teaches strategy to non-players without removing depth. The
method favors learning by doing, gradual introduction, few words, passive and adaptive messages,
low information noise, and making a unit's function clear from its appearance or first action.

Players initially failed to value resource-producing sunflowers, so the problem could not be solved
by a tooltip alone; it affected onboarding and wider balance. Costs, cooldown state, safe early
decisions, and previewed enemy types help players form strategy through play.
