# Source: Dynamic Difficulty Adjustment in Computer Games — A Review

- **Author:** Zohaib et al.
- **Published:** 2018
- **URL:** https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1155/2018/5681652
- **Retrieved:** 2026-07-11

## Summary

The review describes DDA as real-time modification of game features based on player skill, intended to
avoid boredom from excessive simplicity and frustration from excessive difficulty. It also identifies
the risk that adaptation can produce negative experience when players perceive a predetermined or
unfair learning curve.
