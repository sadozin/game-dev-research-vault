# Source: Huch et al. — The Definition-Context-Purpose Paradigm

- **Author:** Huch et al.
- **Published:** 2021
- **URL:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2110.04148
- **Retrieved:** 2026-07-11

## Summary

This interview study asks experienced developers how they define and use quests and narratives. Its
value for implementation is the reminder that a quest is not only a checklist of conditions: context,
purpose, player motivation, and the relationship between objective and story affect whether the system
feels meaningful. The tracker should support the design intent instead of reducing every quest to a
generic counter.
