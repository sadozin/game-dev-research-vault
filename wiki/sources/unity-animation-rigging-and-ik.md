# Source: Unity Animation Rigging and inverse kinematics

- **Author:** Unity Technologies
- **Published:** continuously maintained documentation
- **URL:** https://docs.unity3d.com/Packages/com.unity.animation.rigging@1.3/manual/index.html
- **Retrieved:** 2026-07-11

## Summary

Animation rigs apply constraints such as two-bone IK, aim, and multi-parent relationships after or
alongside authored animation. IK is valuable for contact and aiming, but constraints add evaluation
cost and can expose bad targets, unreachable poses, or conflicting priorities. A production rig needs
clear spaces, weights, limits, and fallback animation.
