## Summary

The official guide presents a competitive tower-defense variant in which hero positioning, tower
upgrades, spells, and progression operate in a PvP context. Competitive play makes clarity,
determinism, matchmaking, synchronization, anti-cheat, and balance cadence product requirements.

PvP is therefore a separate production track, not a mode to bolt onto a single-player simulation at
the end. Even asynchronous “send enemies” designs create adversarial economy and snowball problems
that do not exist in a fixed authored campaign.
