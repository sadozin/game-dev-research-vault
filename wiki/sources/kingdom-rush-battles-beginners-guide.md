## What the source says

The official guide presents a competitive tower-defense variant in which hero positioning, tower
upgrades, spells, and progression operate in a PvP context.

## Derived production implication

Competitive play adds synchronization, matchmaking, adversarial balance, anti-cheat considerations,
and an ongoing balance cadence. PvP is therefore a separate production track rather than a mode to
bolt onto a single-player simulation. These are engineering inferences from the competitive feature,
not statements made by the beginner guide.
