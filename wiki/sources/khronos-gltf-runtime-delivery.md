## Summary

Khronos defines glTF as a royalty-free specification for efficient transmission and loading of 3D
scenes and models. Its design minimizes asset size and runtime unpacking work while remaining
extensible and interoperable across tools and applications. That focus is a delivery boundary, not a
claim that every authoring feature is represented identically.
