## Summary

Screen-space and texture-driven techniques approximate lighting, material, and motion detail by using
available buffers or precomputed data. They are attractive because they avoid geometry or expensive
world-wide computation, but their quality is bounded by screen coverage, history, bandwidth, and the
cost of evaluating many animated instances.
