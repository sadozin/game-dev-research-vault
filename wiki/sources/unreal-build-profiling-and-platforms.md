## Summary

Unreal packaging separates build, cook, stage, and package operations; Unreal Insights records traces;
HLOD reduces large-world draw and actor complexity; Sequencer authors time-based cinematics; C++
gameplay uses reflected types; Android packaging depends on SDKs, formats, and device constraints.
Each boundary is valuable only when the shipped target is tested rather than inferred from the editor.
