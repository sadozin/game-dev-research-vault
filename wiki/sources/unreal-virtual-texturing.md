## Summary

Epic distinguishes Streaming Virtual Texturing, whose cooked texels are loaded from disk, from Runtime
Virtual Texturing, whose texels are generated by the GPU. Both cache texel data on demand and support
larger textures with a more consistent runtime footprint. The documentation positions SVT for large
artist-created textures, lightmaps, and UDIMs, while RVT suits procedural or composited landscape and
decal-like material data.
