## Summary

Unreal’s Landscape tools author terrain, UMG builds widget interfaces, Data Assets hold reusable
design data, and the Online Subsystem abstracts platform services. The common benefit is a named
authoring or integration boundary; the common catch is that asset lifetime, platform support, and
runtime ownership must be tested rather than assumed from editor success.
