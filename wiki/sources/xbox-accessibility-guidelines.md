# Source: Xbox accessibility guidelines

- **Author:** Microsoft
- **Published:** continuously maintained guidance
- **URL:** https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/gaming/accessibility/xbox-accessibility-guidelines
- **Retrieved:** 2026-07-11

## Summary

The guidelines frame accessibility as a set of barriers to remove across input, vision, hearing,
cognition, and motor control. Remapping, readable presentation, captions, difficulty options, and
redundant signals are design requirements rather than a single “accessibility mode.” Options can
still fail when they are hidden, incomplete, or applied only to menus and not gameplay.
