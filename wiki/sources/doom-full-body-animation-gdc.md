# Source: Bringing Hell to Life - AI and Full Body Animation in DOOM

- **Author:** Jake Campbell, id Software
- **Published:** GDC 2017 AI Summit
- **URL:** https://www.gdcvault.com/play/1024186/Bringing-Hell-to-Life-AI
- **Retrieved:** 2026-07-14
- **Raw:** raw/doom-full-body-animation-gdc.md (provenance only)

## Summary

Campbell explains that Doom's stylized combat characters depend on clear full-body animation and that
making straightforward combat AI work dynamically requires robust and flexible animation controls,
not naive clip playback.

The reusable implication is an explicit attack timeline: anticipation, commitment, damage or
projectile event, recovery, and interrupt windows. Navigation, animation transitions, warping, and
damage timing must preserve the same readable contract at combat speed.
