# Source: Epic — Blueprint Best Practices

- **Author:** Epic Games
- **Published:** Undated (Unreal Engine 5.8 documentation)
- **URL:** https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/unreal-engine/blueprint-best-practices
- **Retrieved:** 2026-07-11

## Summary

Epic recommends Blueprints for prototyping, event-driven functionality, and designer iteration, while
calling out large per-frame operations and complex math as cases where native C++ may be preferable.
It presents the decision as workload-dependent and encourages structuring graphs so they remain
readable and maintainable.
