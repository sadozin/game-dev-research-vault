# Source: Ma et al. — Monetization mechanisms in gacha games

- **Author:** Ma et al.
- **Published:** 2025
- **URL:** https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S1875952125001247
- **Retrieved:** 2026-07-11

## Summary

This study examines pricing, pity systems, and beliefs about luck as connected parts of gacha
monetization. It describes pity as a guarantee after a number of unsuccessful pulls and shows why the
advertised single-pull probability does not fully describe the player's chance distribution once pity
states and pricing are included. The useful design implication is to evaluate the complete mechanism,
not optimize rate, price, or guarantee independently.
