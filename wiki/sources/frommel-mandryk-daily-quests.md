# Source: Frommel and Mandryk — Daily Quests or Daily Pests?

- **Author:** Julian Frommel and Regan L. Mandryk
- **Published:** 2022, Proceedings of the ACM on Human-Computer Interaction, volume 6
- **URL:** https://doi.org/10.1145/3549489
- **Retrieved:** 2026-07-11

## Summary

This mixed-methods study examines daily login rewards, repeatable challenges, and seasonal rewards.
Players described them as helpful motivation in some cases, but as negative, fear-of-missing-out
pressure, or an obligation in others. The result is a warning against treating engagement rewards as
universally positive: task effort, reward meaning, player motivation, and perceived pressure shape the
experience.
