# Source: What Features Influence Impact Feel?

- **Author:** The authors of the impact-feedback study
- **Published:** 2022
- **URL:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2208.06155
- **Retrieved:** 2026-07-11

## Summary

The study investigates which features of action-game feedback influence players' perception of impact,
using player commentary and a model of “impact feel.” It supports treating hit feedback as a designed,
multi-feature experience rather than equating one effect such as screen shake with satisfying combat.
