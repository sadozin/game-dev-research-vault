- **Author:** Blender Foundation

## Summary

Blender documents baking textures, normal maps, AO, and lightmaps for export and runtime use. A bake
requires a UV map and an Image Texture or Color Attribute target, and its result depends on render
settings such as samples and bounces. The manual also warns about hard splits that can create nonsmooth
normal results.
