## Summary

Epic compares Blueprint and C++ as complementary tools. Blueprints provide an accessible visual
representation for scripting and C++ compiles to native machine code, while Blueprint-specific C++
markup lets programmers create systems that designers extend. The guide recommends deciding based on
the project's needs and notes that most projects benefit from a mixture.
