## Summary

Epic presents Lumen as a dynamic global-illumination and reflection system for detailed environments
from millimeters to kilometers. It describes diffuse interreflection, indirect specular reflections,
emissive and sky lighting, translucency, foliage, and the relationship between Lumen and precomputed
static lighting. Enabling Lumen disables the scene's precomputed static-lighting contributions and
requires the project to choose Lumen for dynamic GI and reflections.
