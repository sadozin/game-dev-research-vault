## Summary

Guild Wars 2 demonstrates a constrained-loadout model. Its first five skill-bar slots are set by
the character's profession and equipped weapon, while healing, utility, and elite slots allow
player choice. Each profession also owns a unique mechanic and resource, adding identity beyond
the shared bar shape. Changing weapon changes the available weapon skills, so gear selection is
an active build decision rather than a pure stat upgrade.

The model bounds moment-to-moment complexity while preserving variety across professions,
weapons, traits, and selected utility skills. It is a useful counterexample to giving every
learned skill to the player simultaneously.
