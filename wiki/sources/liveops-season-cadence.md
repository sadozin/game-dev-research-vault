## Summary

Live-ops cadence is a production system connecting content, events, communication, measurement, and
operations. A season gives players a legible time window, but cadence must fit the team’s ability to
ship and support content. Too-fast cycles create fatigue and technical debt; too-slow cycles make
returning players feel there is no reason to come back.
