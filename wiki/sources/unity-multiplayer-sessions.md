## Summary

Lobby and relay services separate discovery, membership, connection data, and transport fallback.
Session design must define ownership, joinability, readiness, disconnect handling, and cleanup; a
lobby record alone is not a running game. Services simplify reachability and coordination while
adding quotas, latency, authentication, and failure states.
