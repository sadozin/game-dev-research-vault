## Summary

Epic's LEGO Action Adventure example uses quests to track story progression and stores quest progress
in persistent data. Actions can award resources that unlock areas, fulfill quest requirements, and
unlock trophies. The example illustrates the useful boundary between gameplay events, objective
tracking, persistent state, and rewards.
