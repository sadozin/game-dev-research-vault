## Summary

Unreal’s physics documentation describes rigid-body simulation, collision, constraints, and physics
assets as systems with distinct authoring and runtime responsibilities. Complexity, collision fidelity,
solver settings, and network authority determine whether a physically plausible scene is also a stable
gameplay system.
