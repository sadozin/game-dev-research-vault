## Summary

Blender documents remeshing as regenerating a mesh with approximately the same shape but different
face count or topology. Voxel remeshing creates a regular grid-like surface and is useful for cleanup
or sculpt preparation, while the manual cautions that such topology is not a substitute for every
retopology goal. Snapping can align a new mesh to the original.
