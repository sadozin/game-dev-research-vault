# Source: Auction House Behavior in MMOG Glitch

- **Author:** The authors of the Glitch auction-house study
- **Published:** 2016
- **URL:** https://arxiv.org/abs/1603.07610
- **Retrieved:** 2026-07-11

## Summary

The study analyzes auction-house data across the lifetime of the MMOG Glitch and examines how players
used the market over time. It demonstrates why market design should be evaluated from behavioral logs,
including participation, success, and changing activity, rather than only from intended rules.
