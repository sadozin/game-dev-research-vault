# Source: Apple — Onboarding

- **Author:** Apple
- **Published:** Undated
- **URL:** https://developer.apple.com/design/human-interface-guidelines/onboarding
- **Retrieved:** 2026-07-11

## Summary

Apple recommends making onboarding fast, fun, and optional when it is necessary, with a way to skip it
and find it again later. The guidance supports contextual learning and respecting users who already
understand the product.
