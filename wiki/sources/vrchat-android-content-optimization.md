- **Published:** Undated

## Summary

VRChat recommends keeping Quest avatars to very few materials and restricts avatar shaders on Android
to the VRChat Mobile family. The guide warns that alpha fill rate is a significant mobile-GPU cost,
recommends avoiding transparency where possible, and advises profiling on representative hardware.
