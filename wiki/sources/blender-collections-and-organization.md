## Summary

Blender Collections organize objects without imposing parent-child transforms and support visibility,
linking, appending, instancing, and export workflows. Objects can belong to multiple collections, and
removing an object from one collection does not necessarily remove its data from the scene. Cleanup
operations must therefore distinguish membership from ownership and deletion.
