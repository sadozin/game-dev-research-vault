- **Author:** Amit Patel

- **Retrieved:** 2026-07-11

## Summary

Heightmaps encode terrain elevation as a scalar field that can be sampled, filtered, eroded, and
converted into meshes or terrain tiles. Noise alone produces texture, not believable landforms;
continental shape, drainage, slopes, and gameplay constraints need separate layers.
