## What the source says

Bloons TD 6 advertises three upgrade paths per tower, activated abilities, heroes with their own
progression, four-player co-op, many handcrafted maps, meta-upgrades, events, regular content updates,
offline play, and optional real-money purchases.

## Derived production implication

The game represents the roster/progression/live-content lineage of classic path defense. Its breadth
depends on mature content, balance, event, persistence, and co-op pipelines; those are not a sensible
starting feature count for a first project.
