# Reward Schedules and Variable Ratio

A reward schedule defines how player actions map to rewards: fixed or variable, and by ratio of actions
or passage of time. Variable-ratio rewards create uncertainty, but uncertainty is a pacing tool, not a
substitute for meaningful progress or fair disclosure.

## Why it works

Fixed rewards communicate milestones; variable rewards add surprise and can sustain attention when the
underlying activity remains enjoyable. Mixing guaranteed progress with bounded variance preserves both
agency and anticipation.

## The catch

Unbounded or opaque variance can feel manipulative, create grind, and make players unable to plan.
Repeated reward checks can also crowd out intrinsic goals and distort telemetry toward compulsive use.

## In practice here

Publish odds or guarantees where relevant, add pity or milestone floors, monitor attempts and frustration,
and ensure the expected value is not the only path to meaningful progression.
