# IAP Pricing Tiers

IAP pricing tiers are a small, legible set of purchase points and bundle sizes that map real money to
clear player value. Good tiers reduce choice friction without disguising cost or forcing players to
buy more currency than they need.

## Why it works

Anchors, a low-cost entry point, and larger value bundles let different cohorts choose their comfort
level. Region-aware storefront pricing and consistent contents make the offer understandable and
reduce arbitrage or accidental price discrepancies.

## The catch

Virtual currency bundles create leftover balances and obscure the effective price. Aggressive anchoring,
limited-time pressure, or required premium currency can undermine trust and create policy risk.

## In practice here

Show the cash price and contents before purchase, test conversion by cohort, keep bundle value monotonic,
and ensure the game remains usable without buying the largest tier. Treat store policy and regional tax
or price behavior as product requirements.
