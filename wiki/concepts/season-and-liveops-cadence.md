# Season and live-ops cadence

Live-ops cadence is the rhythm by which a game plans, ships, measures, communicates, and retires
events or content.

## Why it works

Predictable windows help players understand when to return and help teams schedule production,
testing, marketing, and support around a shared release boundary.

## The catch

Cadence is a capacity constraint, not only a calendar. Frequent resets can exhaust players and staff;
slow or shallow seasons expose the absence of meaningful content.

## In practice

Set cadence from sustainable content throughput, reserve time for fixes, and publish rules for catch-up,
retirement, and rewards before a season begins.
