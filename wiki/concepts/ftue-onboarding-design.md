# First-time user experience onboarding

FTUE is the opening sequence that establishes controls, goals, feedback, and the reason to continue.

## Why it works

Short learn-do-feedback loops build a player’s mental model while preserving the reward of play.
Contextual teaching is easier to remember than a disconnected list of instructions.

## The catch

Teaching too much delays the core loop; teaching too little leaves players unable to interpret goals
or failure. A tutorial can also conceal whether the real game is fun.

## In practice

Instrument abandonment by step, let players retry without punishment, and teach only what the next
minute of play requires.
