# Battle pass design

A battle pass is a time-bounded progression track that exchanges play or purchase for staged rewards.

## Why it works

Visible tiers give a season a legible goal and let content cadence, challenges, and rewards reinforce
the same return loop.

## The catch

Poor pacing turns optional engagement into fear of missing out. Paid acceleration, repetitive tasks,
and weak free rewards can damage trust more than they improve conversion.

## In practice

Model completion time for different player cohorts, keep progress understandable, and design catch-up
rules before the first season ships.
